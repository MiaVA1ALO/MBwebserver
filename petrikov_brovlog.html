Petrikov Brovlog, the man who never could

This was my ex and dead husband.. Petrikov Brovlog. For 18 years I carry the Brovlog last name.... but no more (He has died for those unaware).

He hath given me the seed which birthed 4 out of 5 of my beautiful babies. But every man loses purpose after time - and Petrikov already had very little (Purpose). Goodbye Petrikov Brovlog.
